Trivia Round I

The Iron Throne — Seasons 1–2

Q1

How many direwolf pups do the Stark children find in the pilot episode?

Hint: Think of the number of Stark children — including the bastard.

Q2

What does "Valar Morghulis" mean in High Valyrian, and what is the traditional reply?

Hint: It is the creed of the Faceless Men of Braavos.

Q3

Who said: "Never forget what you are. The rest of the world will not. Wear it like armor and it can never be used to hurt you."

Hint: Said to Jon Snow on the road to the Wall in Season 1.

Q4

Where and how were Daenerys's three dragon eggs finally hatched?

Hint: Fire plays a central role — and Dany walked through the flames.

Deep Lore

Dragons, Magic & the Supernatural

🐉
Drogon
Named after Khal Drogo. The largest dragon. Carried Daenerys from the Meereen fighting pits. S5E09
🔥
Rhaegal & Viserion
Viserion was killed by the Night King's ice spear and reanimated as an undead ice dragon. S7E06
❄️
White Walkers
Created by the Children of the Forest. Killed only by dragonglass or Valyrian steel. S6E05
⚔️
Valyrian Steel
Forged in dragonfire in Old Valyria. Longclaw (Jon), Heartsbane (Tarlys), Oathkeeper (Brienne) are key blades.
🧿
Warging
Entering the minds of animals. Bran Stark is the most powerful warg, becoming the Three-Eyed Raven. S3E01
🌳
Weirwood Trees
Sacred heart trees of the Old Gods. The Night King was destroyed at the Winterfell weirwood. S8E03

Trivia Round II

Deaths, Betrayals & Red Weddings

Q5

Who delivered the fatal stab to Robb Stark at the Red Wedding, and what famous line accompanied it?

Hint: He was a lord who betrayed the Starks in exchange for power over the North.

Q6

How was King Joffrey Baratheon killed at his own wedding, and who orchestrated it?

Hint: The method was hidden in his wine goblet. Two schemers from different houses.

Q7

What dangerous substance did Tyrion deploy against Stannis's fleet at the Battle of the Blackwater?

Hint: An alchemical green flame that cannot be extinguished by water.

Q8

Which house was given control of the North as a reward after the Red Wedding?

Hint: Their sigil is a flayed man. They later became infamous for Ramsay.

Who killed the Night King, and with what weapon?

Hint: A young Stark girl. The weapon was a Valyrian steel dagger with a storied history.

Hint: Not all three are Valyrian steel. One was gifted by a Lord Commander.

Trivia Round III

The Wall, Jon Snow & Beyond

Q11

Who is famous for saying "You know nothing, Jon Snow," and what happened to them?

Hint: A Wildling woman from beyond the Wall who became Jon's lover.

Q12

How was Jon Snow resurrected after being murdered by his own Night's Watch brothers?

Hint: A Red Priestess performed a ritual involving washing and incantation.

Q13

What is the origin story of Hodor's name, and how did he ultimately die?

Hint: It happened in a cave, through Bran's warging ability — past and present collided.

Q14

What army arrived to save Jon Snow at the last moment in the Battle of the Bastards?

Hint: Sansa had secretly contacted their lord — who also had feelings for her mother.

Trivia Round IV

The Final Season — Endings & Legacies

Q15

Who destroyed the Iron Throne, how, and what did they do afterward?

Hint: It wasn't a human — and it acted out of apparent grief.

Q16

Who was ultimately crowned ruler of Westeros in the series finale, and how were they chosen?

Hint: Not the most obvious candidate — chosen at a council gathering in King's Landing.

Q17

What explosive act did Cersei commit in the Season 6 finale to destroy all her enemies at once?

Hint: It involved the Great Sept and an alchemical substance stockpiled beneath the city.

What is Jon Snow's true name and birthright, and who are his real parents?

Hint: His parentage was revealed via Bran's visions — he has a stronger claim to the throne than Daenerys.
